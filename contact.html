Response Time Expectation

Our engineering and support team reviews all incoming inquiries during standard business hours (Monday through Friday, 9:00 AM – 5:00 PM EST). We aim to provide an initial response to all technical support and bug report emails within 24–48 hours. Please note that during high traffic periods, resolution of complex rendering bugs may take additional time while our developers investigate the specific DOM extraction constraints.
