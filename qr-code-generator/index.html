Frequently Asked Questions

Is this QR code generator completely free?

Yes, absolutely. Unlike many other tools that charge you to download high-resolution QR codes or make them expire, our generator is 100% free with no hidden limits. You can generate as many QR codes as you want, and they will never expire.

Do these QR codes expire?

No, the QR codes generated here are static QR codes. This means the actual URL or text is embedded directly into the pattern of the code. Because we don't route the link through our servers (no redirects), the QR code will work forever as long as your target URL remains active.

Is my data secure?

Yes. This tool runs entirely client-side in your browser. The data you enter to generate the QR code is never sent to our servers or stored anywhere. It is completely private and secure.

Can I use these QR codes for commercial purposes?

Yes! You can use the generated QR codes on business cards, flyers, restaurant menus, product packaging, and anywhere else. They are yours to use commercially without any attribution required.
