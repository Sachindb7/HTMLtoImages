Why Convert WebP to PNG?

Converting WebP to PNG ensures maximum compatibility across all platforms and applications. While WebP is great for the web, many desktop applications, print workflows, and older systems still require PNG format. Our converter preserves full image quality during the conversion.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this WebP to PNG converter free?

Yes, our WebP to PNG converter is 100% free with no hidden limits, no registration required, and no watermarks on your downloaded images.

Are my WebP files uploaded to your servers?

No. The entire WebP to PNG conversion process happens locally in your browser using HTML5 Canvas APIs. Your images are never uploaded to any external server.

Does PNG support transparency?

Yes, WebP and PNG support full transparency (alpha channels), so your transparent backgrounds will be preserved.

What is the maximum file size I can convert?

There is no hard file size limit since all processing happens in your browser. However, very large images (above 20MB) may take longer depending on your device's memory and processing power.

Will converting WebP to PNG reduce image quality?

No. PNG is a lossless format, so converting to PNG preserves full image quality with absolutely zero loss.
